My Favourite Projects

Over my many years of software development, I've worked on hundreds of projects, some lasting just days and others continuing to be supported months or even years after their creation. Here are some of my favourites!
Unlimited YouTube Search Python UYTS (Unlimited YouTube Search) is a small Python library I created to search YouTube without the need for its heavily restrictive Data API v3.
TidesX JavaScript TidesX is a quick and easy web app to check the tide times. It features tide times and predicted heights for 700+ locations, sun and moon information, and more, all inside a beautiful and efficient interface.
Othello TypeScript For a CS project, I was tasked with implementing the classic game Othello. I chose to write an Electron app in TypeScript, complete with online multiplayer and a challenging AI to compete against.
Solar Sailors Python As part of my Exeter Mathematics Certificate, I worked in a group of five developing a simulation of a solar sail going to Mars. This involved writing a basic physics engine in Python as well as team work.
FreeTube Music JavaScript After being frustrated by YouTube Music charging money just to listen in the background, I wrote my own version built around YouTube's iframe API, which I still use to listen to music today.
